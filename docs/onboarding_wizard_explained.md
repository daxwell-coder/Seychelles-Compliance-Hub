# Seychelles Compliance Hub - Onboarding Wizard Explained

## Executive Summary

The **Seychelles Compliance Hub Onboarding Wizard** is a comprehensive 5-step digital process designed to streamline client registration while ensuring full regulatory compliance with Seychelles Financial Services Authority (FSA) requirements and automatic submission to the **FIU Seychelles Beneficial Ownership Database** as mandated by the **Beneficial Ownership Act 2020**. This system replaces traditional paper-based processes with an intelligent, automated workflow that directly integrates with government systems.

---

## 🏢 **FOR COMPANY OWNERS/FOUNDERS**

### **What This System Does For You:**

The onboarding wizard automates your compliance journey, making it faster, more accurate, and less stressful to establish your business in Seychelles while meeting all regulatory requirements.

### **The 5-Step Process Breakdown:**

---

## **STEP 1: Company Details** 📋
*"Tell us about your business"*

### **What You'll Provide:**
- **Company Name** - Your legal business name
- **Registration Number** - Your Seychelles company registration number
- **Business Type** - Select from:
  - **IBC** (International Business Company) - Most common for international businesses
  - **CSL** (Companies Special License) - For financial services
  - **Foundation** - For asset protection and succession planning
  - **Partnership/Trust/Fund** - Specialized structures

### **Address Information:**
- **Registered Address** - Your official Seychelles address (required by law)
- **Business Address** - Where you actually operate (can be same or different)

### **Contact & Financial Details:**
- Email, phone, website
- **Authorized Capital** - The maximum share capital your company can issue

### **Why This Matters to You:**
- Establishes your legal identity in Seychelles
- Creates your official record with authorities
- Sets foundation for all future compliance activities
- **Prepares data for automatic FIU BO database submission**

---

## **STEP 2: Regulatory Profile** ⚖️
*"Which rules apply to your business?"*

### **What You'll Configure:**

#### **Primary Regulatory Framework:**
- **FSA Seychelles** - Main financial services regulation
- **ICA 2016** - International Corporate Service Providers Act
- **AML/CFT** - Anti-Money Laundering & Counter-Financing Terrorism
- **GDPR** - Data protection (if handling EU data)
- **CRS** - Common Reporting Standard (tax information exchange)

#### **Business Activities:**
- Detailed description of what your company does
- Industry classification
- Geographic scope of operations

#### **Risk Assessment:**
The system automatically calculates your risk level based on:
- **LOW RISK:** Simple trading, consulting, holding companies
- **MEDIUM RISK:** Multi-jurisdictional operations, higher transaction volumes
- **HIGH RISK:** Complex financial services, high-risk jurisdictions

### **Why This Matters to You:**
- Determines which regulations you must follow
- Sets your compliance obligations and reporting requirements
- Influences approval timelines and ongoing monitoring

---

## **STEP 3: Beneficial Owners** 👥
*"Who really owns and controls this company?"*

### **What You'll Declare:**

#### **25% Threshold Rule:**
Anyone who owns **25% or more** of your company must be declared as a beneficial owner.

#### **For Each Beneficial Owner:**
- **Personal Details:** Full name, nationality, date of birth
- **Identification:** Passport number, country of issue
- **Address:** Residential address with proof
- **Ownership Details:** 
  - Percentage of shares owned
  - Type of ownership (direct/indirect)
  - Nature of control exercised

#### **Ultimate Beneficial Owners (UBO):**
- The natural persons who ultimately own/control the company
- Required even through complex ownership structures

### **Why This Matters to You:**
- **Legal Requirement:** Seychelles Beneficial Ownership Act 2020 mandates disclosure
- **Automatic FIU Submission:** System submits directly to FIU BO database (fiu.sc:4443/BO_Live)
- **Banking:** Banks require this information to open accounts
- **Compliance:** Essential for AML/CFT compliance under current regulations
- **Transparency:** Builds trust with regulators and business partners
- **Resident Agent Compliance:** Ensures your resident agent meets FIU reporting obligations

---

## **STEP 4: Document Upload** 📄
*"Prove everything with official documents"*

### **Required Documents:**

#### **Corporate Documents:**
- **Certificate of Incorporation** - Proof your company exists
- **Memorandum & Articles** - Your company's constitution
- **Register of Directors** - Current director information
- **Good Standing Certificate** - Company is in good legal standing

#### **Beneficial Owner Documents (for each person >25%):**
- **Passport Copy** - Government-issued identification
- **Utility Bill** - Proof of residential address (last 3 months)
- **Bank Reference** - Character reference from your bank
- **Professional Reference** - From lawyer, accountant, or business associate

#### **Compliance Documents:**
- **AML Policy** - Your anti-money laundering procedures
- **Beneficial Owner Declaration** - Sworn statement of ownership

### **Technical Requirements:**
- **File Size:** Maximum 10MB per document
- **Formats:** PDF, JPG, JPEG, PNG only
- **Quality:** Documents must be clear and fully legible

### **Why This Matters to You:**
- **Verification:** Proves all information provided is accurate
- **Legal Compliance:** Required by Seychelles corporate law
- **Due Diligence:** Enables banks and partners to work with you
- **Audit Trail:** Creates permanent record for authorities

---

## **STEP 5: Review & Submit** ✅
*"Final check before official submission"*

### **What Happens:**
- **Complete Review:** All information displayed for verification
- **Edit Capability:** Can return to any previous step to make changes
- **Validation Check:** System verifies all requirements are met
- **Digital Signature:** Electronic submission with timestamp
- **Confirmation:** Immediate receipt and tracking number

### **After Submission:**
- **Processing Status:** Real-time tracking of application progress
- **FSA Review:** Automatic forwarding to regulatory authorities
- **Notifications:** Email updates on approval status
- **Account Activation:** Access to ongoing compliance dashboard
