## Risk Level Examples - Real Seychelles Scenarios

### 🚨 **CRITICAL RISK** Examples
**Triggers 2-hour emergency response**

1. **"FSA suspends all new IBC registrations due to FATF concerns"**
   - System Action: Immediate alert to CEO + compliance team
   - Business Impact: Stop all new client onboarding immediately
   - Required Response: Board meeting, external legal counsel

2. **"Enhanced due diligence required within 48 hours for all existing IBCs"**
   - System Action: Calculate affected clients (e.g., 150 IBCs)  
   - Business Impact: Need 3 additional staff for 2 weeks
   - Required Response: Client notifications, process documentation

3. **"AML violations result in criminal referrals - specific procedures cited"**
   - System Action: Cross-reference your current procedures
   - Business Impact: Immediate procedure review and updates
   - Required Response: Legal review, staff retraining

### ⚠️ **HIGH RISK** Examples  
**Triggers 24-hour planned response**

1. **"New beneficial ownership reporting forms - effective January 1st"**
   - System Action: Identify all clients needing updates
   - Business Impact: 30 days to update 200+ client files
   - Required Response: Staff training, client communications

2. **"KYC documentation requirements expanded to include source of wealth"**
   - System Action: Gap analysis of current client files
   - Business Impact: Additional documentation for 80% of clients  
   - Required Response: Process updates, client requests

### 📋 **MEDIUM RISK** Examples
**Triggers 3-day systematic response**

1. **"Annual filing fees increase by 10% starting April 1st"**
   - System Action: Update fee schedules, client agreements
   - Business Impact: Client communications, system updates
   - Required Response: Administrative updates

2. **"Electronic filing portal maintenance scheduled for weekend"**
   - System Action: Schedule filings around maintenance window
   - Business Impact: Adjust filing calendar for that week
   - Required Response: Operational planning

### 📝 **LOW RISK** Examples
**Automatic filing, no immediate action**

1. **"FSA holiday schedule updated for 2025"**
   - System Action: Update internal calendars
   - Business Impact: Plan filings around holidays
   - Required Response: Administrative note

2. **"New FSA contact information for public inquiries"**
   - System Action: Update contact database
   - Business Impact: None immediate
   - Required Response: Reference update

## How the System Calculates Risk

### **Automatic Risk Scoring**

The system looks at each regulatory change and asks:

1. **Urgency Keywords?**
   - "Immediate" = +50 risk points
   - "Within 48 hours" = +40 risk points  
   - "Next month" = +20 risk points
   - "Next year" = +5 risk points

2. **Impact Scope?**
   - "All licensed entities" = +40 risk points
   - "IBCs only" = +30 risk points
   - "New applications" = +20 risk points
   - "Administrative" = +5 risk points

3. **Compliance Area?**
   - "Money laundering" = +45 risk points
   - "Beneficial ownership" = +35 risk points
   - "Filing requirements" = +25 risk points
   - "Office procedures" = +5 risk points

4. **Consequences Mentioned?**
   - "License revocation" = +50 risk points
   - "Penalties" = +40 risk points
   - "Additional requirements" = +20 risk points
   - "Information only" = +0 risk points

### **Risk Score Calculation**
```
Total Points:
- 80-100 points = CRITICAL (immediate action)
- 60-79 points  = HIGH (same day action)  
- 40-59 points  = MEDIUM (this week action)
- 0-39 points   = LOW (file for reference)
```

### **Confidence Factor**
The system also considers how confident it is:
- High confidence + High risk = Immediate escalation
- Low confidence + High risk = Human review required
- High confidence + Low risk = Automatic processing
