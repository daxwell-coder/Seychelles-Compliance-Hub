- **Total Seychelles IBCs**: ~15,000 active companies
- **Non-Compliant Estimate**: 60-70% (9,000-10,500 companies)
- **Serviceable Market**: $750-$900 per onboarding + $150/month ongoing
- **Annual Revenue Potential**: $27M+ (onboarding) + $27M+ (recurring)

#### **🔴 High-Priority Segments**
1. **Overdue Filers** (2,000-3,000 companies)

   - Willing to pay premium for urgent service
   - **Revenue**: $2.25M-$3.375M (onboarding)

2. **Foreign-Owned IBCs** (8,000-10,000 companies)
   - Owners lack local compliance knowledge

   - Strong recurring revenue potential
   - **Revenue**: $9M-$12M (onboarding)

3. **Financial Services IBCs** (1,500-2,000 companies)
   - Highest compliance requirements
   - Premium pricing potential
   - **Revenue**: $2.25M-$3M (onboarding)

#### **🟡 Medium-Priority Segments**
4. **Multi-Jurisdiction IBCs** (3,000-4,000 companies)
   - Operating in multiple countries
   - Complex compliance needs
   - **Revenue**: $3.375M-$4.5M (onboarding)

5. **Aging IBCs** (5,000+ companies incorporated 5+ years ago)
   - Outdated compliance practices
   - Beneficial ownership verification needed
   - **Revenue**: $5.625M+ (onboarding)
